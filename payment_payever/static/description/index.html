payever Checkout — Composable Payment Gateway. For Free.

With payever Checkout you can offer your customers a variety of payment methods — from Buy Now Pay Later, Installments & Financing to Credit Card payments, PayPal, local payment methods and real time bank transfers based on Open Banking technologies — all with just one plugin.

Highlights

Buy Now Pay Later

Pay in 14 or 30 days, split in up to 36 monthly installments as well as traditional financing with best terms and conditions for both customers and merchants. We offer different types of Buy Now Pay Later products — in cooperation with our partners Santander and Openbank.

Sustainability

Together with our exclusive partner Ivy, you and your customers can support certified climate protection projects with every payment — without any additional costs.

All Payment Options

With payever Checkout you can offer your customers all common payment options via just one partner — without having to install and maintain multiple plugins.

Free of Charge

payever is not charging any setup or transaction fees — you only bear the costs of the respective payment provider or bank. You can even benefit from lower partner conditions for specific payment options.

Checkout Branding

You can completely customize your payever Checkout — upload your logo and adjust colors, fields as well as buttons so that the checkout fits your online shop perfectly.

Features

Point of Sale

All payment methods can also be used completely digital in your local store — as a Self-Checkout via QR codes or SMS/email and as a merchant version via the payever platform or integration into your existing PoS system.

Pay by Link

The payment link (or the QR code) can be sent via e-mail or SMS to your customers and works with all payment methods. Ideal for merchants who want to make special offers or process call center orders.

Order Management

The plugin allows you to control all transactions including after-sales actions in your shop backend as well as in your payever account.

Specific Settings

Activate or deactivate payment methods with just one click. Select different payment options depending on shopping cart size or on customer address. Offer the same payment method in different variations.

Widgets & Rate Calculator

Specific widgets can be implemented to provide more information or calculate the monthly rate directly on the product page for installments, financing and Ivy.

Product Synchronization

Easily synchronize your products via payever to other sales channels such as marketplaces (Amazon, eBay), comparison platforms (Google Shopping) and social media (Facebook, Instagram).

Cyber Security

We guarantee the highest security standards using the latest technology and periodic PCI scans as well as annual external audits, based on our close cooperation with partners from the highly regulated banking sector.

GDPR Compliant

All data is stored on ISO/IEC 27001 & PCI DSS certified servers located in the European Union, meeting the highest compliance and security standards.

Customer Support

Our customer support is German and English speaking and helps you with the integration or any technical issues. On weekdays between 9 am and 6 pm, you will receive an immediate response to any question.
