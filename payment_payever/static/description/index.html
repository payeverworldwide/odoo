payever Checkout — Composable Payment Gateway. For Free.

With payever Checkout you can offer your customers a variety of payment methods — from Buy Now Pay Later, Installments & Financing to Credit Card payments, PayPal, local payment methods and real time bank transfers based on Open Banking technologies — all with just one plugin.

Highlights

Buy Now Pay Later

Pay in 14 or 30 days, split in up to 36 monthly installments as well as traditional financing with best terms and conditions for both customers and merchants — in cooperation with our partners Santander and Openbank.

Sustainability

Together with our exclusive partner Ivy, you and your customers can support certified climate protection projects with every payment — without any additional costs.

All Payment Options

Offer all common payment options via just one partner — without having to install and maintain multiple plugins.

Free of Charge

payever charges no setup or transaction fees — you only bear the costs of the respective payment provider or bank. Benefit from lower partner conditions for specific payment options.

Checkout Branding

Completely customize your payever Checkout — upload your logo and adjust colors, fields and buttons so the checkout fits your online shop perfectly.

Features

Point of Sale

All payment methods work digitally in your local store — as Self-Checkout via QR codes or SMS/email, or as a merchant version via the payever platform or your existing PoS system.

Pay by Link

Send a payment link or QR code via e-mail or SMS. Ideal for special offers, call center orders, or API integration into existing processes.

Order Management

Control all transactions including after-sales actions in your shop backend as well as in your payever account.

Specific Settings

Activate or deactivate payment methods with one click. Select options by cart size or customer address. Offer the same method in different variations.

Widgets & Rate Calculator

Implement widgets to show installment or financing rates directly on the product page for installments, financing and Ivy.

Product Synchronization

Synchronize your products via payever to marketplaces (Amazon, eBay), comparison platforms (Google Shopping) and social media (Facebook, Instagram).

Cyber Security

Highest security standards using the latest technology, periodic PCI scans and annual external audits through close cooperation with banking sector partners.

GDPR Compliant

All data is stored on ISO/IEC 27001 & PCI DSS certified servers located in the European Union.

Customer Support

German and English speaking support team. On weekdays between 9 am and 6 pm, you will receive an immediate response to any question.

Our Payment Methods

Buy Now Pay Later

Together with our partners Santander, Openbank and Swedbank we offer exclusive Buy Now Pay Later options: invoice, installments and installment loans.

Santander BNPL

  • Payment period: 30 days
  • Special Feature: No loan agreement or identity check necessary
  • Availability: Germany, Norway

Santander BNPL / Installments

  • Payment period: 3–36 months (0%–7.9% customer interest)
  • Special Feature: No loan agreement or identity check necessary
  • Availability: Germany

Santander Financing / Installments

  • Payment period: 6–72 months (0% financing possible)
  • Special Feature: Fully digital with video legitimation and electronic signing
  • Availability: Germany, Austria, Netherlands, Belgium, Sweden, Denmark, Norway, Finland, Great Britain

Zinia BNPL — Invoice

  • Availability: Germany, Netherlands
  • Special Feature: No loan agreement or identity check necessary

Swedbank Pay Invoice
